PIG GAME

In the Pig game, two players take turns rolling a die. They continue rolling until they get a 1, at which point their turn ends and the other player gets a chance to roll. Players accumulate points during their turn, but if they roll a 1, they lose all the points they've collected in that turn. The first player to reach 100 points wins the game.

Guess My Number Game

In the "Guess My Number" game, a random number is generated, and players must guess what it is. With each incorrect guess, the player loses one point. The game provides hints, indicating whether the guess is too high or too low. The high score is tracked and updated throughout the game. The goal is to guess the number with the fewest attempts to achieve the highest score
